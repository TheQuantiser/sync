=== CERN Kerberos Keytab Setup (Official Method) ===

Goal: Automate CERN login via Kerberos keytab, avoid password prompts.

--- Step 1: SSH into CERN ---
ssh USERNAME@lxplus.cern.ch

--- Step 2: Generate your keytab ---
cern-get-keytab --keytab ~/USERNAME_cern.keytab --user --login USERNAME

→ This saves a valid user keytab in your CERN home dir.

--- Step 3: Copy keytab to your local machine ---
scp USERNAME@lxplus.cern.ch:~/USERNAME_cern.keytab ~/

--- Step 4: Authenticate locally using the keytab ---
kdestroy
kinit -kt ~/USERNAME_cern.keytab USERNAME@CERN.CH
klist

→ If this works, your Kerberos TGT is active and valid.

--- Step 5: SSH into lxplus with Kerberos (no password) ---
ssh USERNAME@lxplus.cern.ch

→ If configured correctly, this should not prompt for a password.

--- Step 6: Verify TGT and AFS forwarding ---
ssh -v USERNAME@lxplus.cern.ch klist -f; tokens; touch ~/.sshtest

→ Should display forwardable TGT and AFS token + write test file.

=== SSH Config (~/.ssh/config) ===

Host lxplus*
    GSSAPIAuthentication yes
    GSSAPIDelegateCredentials yes
    ForwardX11 yes
    ForwardX11Trusted no

→ DO NOT include GSSAPITrustDNS — it is not universally supported.

=== Troubleshooting ===

• If `kinit` fails: double-check the keytab path, filename, permissions.
• If SSH prompts for password: ensure `kinit` succeeded and `klist` shows a TGT.
• If AFS tokens are missing: ensure `GSSAPIDelegateCredentials yes` is present.
